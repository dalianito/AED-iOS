心肺复苏标准操作流程CPR

1. Call For Help

  • Ensure the scene is safe before proceeding.
  • Check the victim for unresponsiveness.
  • If the person is not responsive and not breathing or not breathing normally, call 120 and return to the victim.

2. Begin Chest Compressions

  • Push firmly downwards in the middle of the chest and then release.
  • Push at the rate of 100 compressions per mintute, until help arrives.
  • Let the chest rise completely before pushing down again.
